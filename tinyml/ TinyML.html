TinyML and Sustainability

TinyML is the deployment of machine learning (ML) algorithms onto low-cost, low-power, and resource-constrained MCU systems. This article explores the environmental footprint of TinyML systems and how they can contribute to the UN's environmental sustainability goals.

Summary

  • TinyML can contribute to the UN's environmental sustainability goals by reducing energy consumption, improving energy efficiency, and enabling more efficient use of resources.
  • TinyML can be used to create more efficient and sustainable systems by reducing the amount of data that needs to be processed and transmitted, and by enabling more efficient use of resources.
  • TinyML can be used to create more sustainable products and services by enabling more efficient use of resources, and by reducing the environmental impact of products and services.
