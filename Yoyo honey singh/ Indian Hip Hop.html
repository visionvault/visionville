Summary

Rap music is a powerful form of protest in India. Rappers have been using their music to speak out against social and political issues for decades, and their voices are only getting louder.

The rise of hip-hop in India is due in part to the country's large youth population. Young people are drawn to hip-hop's rebellious spirit and its ability to express their own experiences and perspectives.

Indian rappers are increasingly gaining international recognition. This is due in part to the success of artists like Divine, who has collaborated with international stars like Nas and Major Lazer.
