Autonomous Agents

Autonomous agents are software programs that can perform tasks without human intervention. They are used in a variety of applications, such as customer service, fraud detection, predictive maintenance, and robotics.

Benefits of using autonomous agents

  • Increased efficiency
  • Reduced costs
  • Improved accuracy
  • Increased safety
  • 24/7 availability

Challenges of using autonomous agents

  • Development costs
  • Ethical concerns
  • Safety concerns
  • Legal concerns
